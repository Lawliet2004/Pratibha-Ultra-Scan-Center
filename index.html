About M/S Pratibha Ultra Scan Center

A Decade of Excellence in Diagnostics

Incorporated on April 1st, 2016, M/S Pratibha Ultra Scan Center is a trusted partnership firm with nearly 10 years of experience in providing high-quality medical services in Ranaghat, West Bengal.
